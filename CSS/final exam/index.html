About Zudio

Zudio is a fast-fashion brand from Trent Ltd. (Tata Group) bringing the latest trends in clothing, footwear and beauty at truly affordable prices. With fresh collections dropping every week and stores across India, Zudio makes fashion accessible to everyone.

Our mission is simple: Affordable • Trendy • Everyday Wear. No online app, no heavy ads — just great styles in your nearest store.
